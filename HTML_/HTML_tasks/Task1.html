(3)HTTP/HTTPs:-

1.HTTP:-Hypertext Transfer Protocol

- Transfer the data from one device to another device through the servers.

- Protocol means it has rules and regulations.

- it is not secure ,anybody can steal our data.

2.HTTPs:-Hypertext Tansfer Protocol Secure

- It is same data is secure inside it.

- Hackers can't steal our data.
